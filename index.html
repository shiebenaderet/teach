Teacher-facing · Research-backed

A small library for the teacher next door.

This is the teacher-facing companion to ams.mrbsocialstudies.org. It's a slowly growing collection of classroom resources — plans, protocols, posters, references — written for colleagues, grounded in research, and shaped by what actually holds up in a middle school classroom.

Who’s this

I’m Shie Benaderet, 8th grade Social Studies at Alderwood Middle School. I build these as I go — usually because I needed one and couldn’t find the version I wanted.

Everything here is what I’d want a first-year me, or a colleague down the hall, to have on their desk. If something helps, take it. If it’s wrong or outdated, tell me.
